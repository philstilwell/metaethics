The non-realist reading room

Ideas for a world without a moral realm.

This collection centers views that reject a single, stance-independent moral realm. It also includes related constructivist, relativist, and responsibility-skeptical work whose exact classification is disputed. The authors disagree about moral language, valid judgment, deserved blame, and what we should do next.

The collection presents moral non-realism and closely related debates. Not every constructivist, relativist, or skeptic about basic-desert responsibility uses the label “non-realist.” Inclusion does not mean that The Moral Compass accepts every argument in a paper.
