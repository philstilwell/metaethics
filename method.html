How the diagnostics work

Transparent questions. Limited conclusions.

Every lab is designed to show exactly which answer produced which result. None can prove that a moral theory is true, read a hidden belief, or measure a whole person.

Shared design rules

Six promises every lab should keep

If a lab cannot keep one of these promises, its result should say so rather than hide the problem.

  1. 01

    One question at a time

    Meaning, truth, motivation, authority, usefulness, agreement, and emotional force are kept separate unless a question deliberately compares them.

  2. 02

    Fixed case details

    Each scenario states what to assume and what the question asks. Users are not scored for inventing missing facts.

  3. 03

    Visible coding

    Answer labels and explanations disclose which family receives a marker. No hidden model or AI interprets the response.

  4. 04

    Honest denominators

    A bar means selected ÷ offered, not probability, confidence, virtue, personality, or likelihood that a theory is true.

  5. 05

    Narrow tension rules

    A flagged seam identifies two exact answers that need clarification. It is not automatically a contradiction or proof of irrationality.

  6. 06

    Local privacy

    Answers remain in the open page. Saving a map summary is optional, device-only, erasable, and excludes full answers and AI prompts.

How to read a result

Four labels that do less than they may sound like

Pattern

A repeated answer family in these cases. It is not a permanent identity or complete philosophy.

Skill check

A classification with a defined answer, such as separating a cause from evidence. It does not grade the user’s moral character.

Seam

Two statements whose scopes, sources, or implications deserve comparison. A missing explanation is not yet a formal contradiction.

Conditional result

A conclusion inside the premise stated by the lab. It need not carry over once that premise is rejected.

Quality control

The code checks the claims the prose makes.

Automated audits verify question counts, reachable answer families, denominators, tie handling, result selectors, fixed annotations, AI-prompt context, local links, and exact seam rules. Exhaustive path testing is used where the answer space is tractable. Passing an audit shows that the implementation matches its declared logic—not that the philosophical categories are complete or true.
