# Orientation and Significance Audit

## Mandate

This pass asks a reader-facing question of the complete book: at each stage, can a careful nonspecialist tell where the text is headed, why the present distinction or case matters, what a requested exercise is meant to produce, and what the result leaves open?

The goal is not to annotate every paragraph. Excess signposting can replace an intellectual journey with two simultaneous books—the argument and a running explanation of the argument. The standard is recoverable direction at the points where uncertainty is most costly.

## Scope

The audit covered:

- the prologue and all forty-one numbered chapters;
- all seven part dividers and six Inspection Reports;
- 511 level-two sections and 733 level-three subsections across the narrative and appendices;
- every principal Candidate System exercise, pressure exercise, ledger, charter, dossier, warrant, and inspection;
- all nineteen Candidate System worksheets;
- all forty-five pressure cards, eighteen repair entries, fourteen Civic Stakes Brief sections, glossary families, and formal conclusion limits.

Each narrative section was checked through its heading, entry movement, local payoff, and transition. Reader-action sections received the stricter bounded-claim check: the requested act, its purpose, what completion can establish, and what it cannot establish.

## Editorial criteria

### Direction

The reader should know the immediate question before technical distinctions accumulate. An opening scene may create curiosity, but the chapter should not require several pages of inference before its task becomes explicit.

### Significance

A distinction earns space by changing what the Candidate System may claim, how a case should be analyzed, which debt becomes visible, or what later step becomes possible. A section should make that consequence recoverable without relying on “this will matter later.”

### Bounded inference

A case or exercise must not let classification become verdict, agreement become truth, causal explanation become justification, practical settlement become objective ranking, or uncertainty become arbitrary permission. The reader should know the exact achievement under inspection.

### Cumulative placement

Chapter transitions should identify the debt or question inherited by the next chamber. Inspection Reports should preserve what survived rather than presenting every unresolved debt as demolition.

### Annotation restraint

Explicit annotations belong where a narrative opening delays orientation or a blank form begins before its function is clear. Sections whose heading, entry, and payoff already disclose their role should remain unburdened.

## Findings and interventions

### Chapter-level route markers

The chapter openings were engaging and the closing transitions generally strong. The recurring weakness was that the route through the chapter often remained implicit until after the first major section. A tailored **Where this chapter is headed** annotation now appears after the opening movement of the prologue and every numbered chapter.

Each marker names:

1. the immediate task;
2. the distinction or construction step at stake;
3. the overreach the step is designed to prevent.

The markers do not announce verdicts the chapter has not yet earned.

### Main-journey exercises

Sixty-three explicit **Purpose** annotations now appear across the prologue and numbered chapters. They precede the major translations, maps, tables, protocols, charters, dossiers, warrants, audits, and Inspection Reports. Each tells the reader what artifact to produce and, where confusion is likely, what completing it does not prove.

This intervention moves significance ahead of effort. Readers no longer need to complete a page before discovering why the page exists.

### Candidate System workbook

The workbook had a clear global introduction, but several individual worksheets began immediately with blanks, tables, or checkboxes. The cover sheet and all nineteen worksheets now begin with a tailored **Purpose** annotation. The annotation connects the standalone page to the cumulative construction and preserves the boundary between recording a target, auditing a structure, and establishing objective truth.

### Civic Stakes Brief

The Civic Stakes Brief also had strong overall use rules but uneven local orientation. Its use rules, cover page, fourteen numbered sections, final decision record, reviewer page, and public summary now each begin with a **Purpose** annotation. The additions explain why jurisdiction, standing, emotional stakes, operational values, evidence, comparison, resources, power, alternatives, safeguards, measures, review, repair, and dissent require separate entries.

### Part transitions

The Part V divider now states the governing fairness rule for the theory-family audit: each candidate receives credit for what it saves and is charged only for what it leaves unsupported. The remaining part dividers already named both the task and the inherited debt clearly.

### Pagination safeguards

The production filter now reserves enough vertical space to keep each route marker with the first major heading and protects major section headings with their opening content. This prevents an orientation note from becoming an orphaned paragraph whose destination appears on the next page.

## Strong structures retained

Several components already met the stricter standard and were left structurally intact:

- the pressure-card deck states fixed facts, exact question, controlled variations, and inference tested;
- the repair guide states strongest reading, accomplishment, debt, pressure questions, and honest narrower form;
- the glossary separates the dimensions on which positions differ before defining terms;
- the formal limits appendix states the target, grounding partition, conditional findings, derived conclusion, and claims not established;
- chapter debt entries and Inspection Report landings already provide strong retrospective and prospective continuity.

## Maintenance rule

Future chapters and substantive revisions should preserve three conventions:

1. one tailored **Where this chapter is headed** marker after the opening movement;
2. a **Purpose** annotation before any reusable form or substantial reader action whose significance is not already explicit;
3. a closing transition that names the next unpaid debt or construction problem.

The annotations should remain brief. If a section needs a paragraph explaining why it exists, the section itself probably needs restructuring.

## Verification

Source-level checks confirm one route marker in each of the forty-two narrative files, a purpose-first opening in all twenty Candidate System workbook sections, and a purpose-first opening in all nineteen Civic Stakes Brief sections. Final pagination, rendering, navigation, EPUB integrity, and artifact checks are recorded in the final proof report after the rebuilt edition is inspected.
