## Architecture finding

The seven-part structure is coherent and should remain intact:

| Section | Function | Approximate words |
| --- | --- | ---: |
| Prologue | Establish the construction adventure and its governing question | 1,900 |
| Part I | Specify objectivity, meaning, and the success conditions | 17,100 |
| Part II | Supply standing, verdict objects, values, guidance, comparison, and consistency | 16,800 |
| Part III | Audit reasons and authority | 21,800 |
| Part IV | Audit evidence and access | 18,000 |
| Part V | Give major theory families and hybrids their strongest hearing | 16,500 |
| Part VI | Test the last repairs and derive the general result | 16,200 |
| Part VII | Inventory what survives and construct the public alternative | 11,900 |
| Appendices | Make the argument reusable and preserve its limits | 17,700 |

The argument advances rather than circling: specification → content → authority → access → candidate theories → general result → constructive redirection. Each Part I–VI landing is an Inspection Report, and each report hands a defined unresolved burden to the next part.

## Length and pacing decisions

The main chapter range is intentionally uneven. Chapters 18, 24, and 37 are long because they close major audits; Chapters 5 and 31 are long because they integrate a multi-room analysis or a full candidate tournament. Splitting them would weaken the experience of arriving at an inspection landing. Their length is retained.

Part V's individual theory chapters are shorter and similarly shaped on purpose. The recurring headings **What the candidate saves** and the numbered Debt entries create a fair hearing protocol. They are argumentative controls, not repetitive padding. Part VII is shorter because it changes from demolition to construction and should feel like emerging from the maze rather than entering a second book.

## Navigation decisions

- Every manuscript chapter now uses one level-one heading containing its number and full title.
- All six Inspection Reports use a consistent numeral, em dash, and title-case pattern.
- The publication-facing contents page displays all seven parts, forty-one chapters, and six appendices.
- Optional routes are organized around questions rather than reader identities. The cumulative route remains explicitly preferred.
- Forward references use prospective language. Backward references assume only material the reader has reached.
- The Chapter 26–27 handoff no longer repeats the same clerk action on both sides of the page turn.

## Repetition audit

No long substantive sentence or paragraph is duplicated across the manuscript. The recurring construction vocabulary—Candidate System, Debt Ledger, pressure case, bridge, selector, and Inspection Report—should remain because it gives the reader a stable set of instruments. Repeated cautions such as “does not by itself” are methodologically justified where they block distinct invalid inferences; they should be varied only when the underlying distinction remains equally explicit.

The deliberate theory-chapter template in Part V and the form language in the appendices are retained. The appendices are reusable artifacts, so compression that removes repeated field instructions would damage their standalone function.

## Terminology and conclusion audit

The manuscript consistently keeps apart:

- objective verdicts and objective systems;
- direct standing, comparative weight, and final outcome;
- causal, motivating, conditional, and aim-independent reasons;
- evidence indicating truth and a stance-independent epistemic reason to believe;
- agreement, legitimacy, jurisdiction, obligation, coercion, and power;
- causal responsibility, accountability practices, and basic desert;
- emotions as caused responses, evaluative appraisals, social expressions, and policy inputs;
- failure to earn the requested system and proof that no possible moral fact exists.

The final redirection remains properly conditional and political rather than metaphysically triumphant. Emotions and values are practical primitives, not infallible data or direct legislation. Evidence, representation, comparison, safeguards, power disclosure, review, and repair remain mandatory between response and policy.

## Publication-boundary audit

The manuscript and appendices remain self-contained. They do not refer to the website, implementation files, interactive exercises as software, diagnostic outputs, scores, or profiles. Internal research and alignment documents may continue to name those sources.
